** HTML5 Video

    <video id="myVideo" width="320" height="240" controls>
        <source src="movie.mp4" type="video/mp4">
        <source src="movie.ogg" type="video/ogg">
    </video>

    -- We can use id="myVideo" and can be used as .play(); etc methods.
